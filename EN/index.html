Frequently Asked Questions

I have no poker experience. Are the lessons suitable for me?

Absolutely! The coaching is built to your level. Whether you're completely new or have basic experience, we'll start with fundamentals and progress at your pace.

How long does it take to reach a professional level?

It depends on lesson frequency, your practice, and personal goals. Some students see major improvements within weeks, while others opt for a longer, deeper journey.

Are sessions in-person or online?

We can arrange lessons online (video calls) or face-to-face, depending on your preference and availability.

Which types of games do we focus on?

Mainly No-Limit Hold’em, but we can explore other variants (Omaha, Mixed Games) upon request.

What if I can't complete all the hours consecutively?

We can spread the hours out over a flexible schedule. If there are special constraints, we'll find a solution.

How do I pay?

Payment can be made in cash, bank transfer, or other online methods. We'll figure out the most convenient way for you.

Are lessons also suitable for advanced players?

Definitely. We can delve into advanced topics like range analysis, GTO, ICM in tournaments, and more, depending on your needs.
